My Returnal Struggle...

May 1 2021

I've never been known to be massochistic but after playing Returnal my first experience with the "roguelike" genre, I'm starting to have doubt my mental sannity. You see, I profoudly hate this game but I love it so much I can't stop playing it...

You play as Selene, an astronaut who just crashed into an unknown planet filled with deadly neon H.R Gigeresque creatures that want to annihilate you at all costs. In most action games you are entitled to a certain degree on intentional ambivalence to death in the form of checkpoints and the ability to save your progress. Not in Returnal... Everytime you die, the poorly cursed Selene wakes up on the exact same crashspot where you begin the game and any progress you have made is completely gone for the most part. Frustation is my middle name, and my last name is despair...

To this day I have not been able to go beyond the 3rd biome (there are 5), I don't know how long it will take me but whatever the outcome, I'm never playing a roguelike again!
